  <h2>What is <b>Reel Talk</b>?</h2>
  
  <h4><b>Reel Talk</b> is an online app designed to help people watch, rate, and discuss movies.
    <ul>
      <li>Can't decide what to watch tonight? <b>See what your friends recommend.</b></li>
      <li>Want to know which online streaming service has your favourite movie? <b>We can tell you!</b></li>
      <li>Just watched a great movie and want to tell everyone you know? <b>Add it to </b>Reel Talk<b> and give it a glowing review.</b></li>
    </ul>
  </h4>
