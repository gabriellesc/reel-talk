What is Reel Talk?

Reel Talk is an online app designed to help people watch, rate, and discuss movies.
  • Can't decide what to watch tonight? See what your friends recommend.
  • Want to know which online streaming service has your favourite movie? We can tell you!
  • Just watched a great movie and want to tell everyone you know? Add it to Reel Talk and give it a glowing review.

Who are we?

A passionate, talented group of people who love movies!

Since Reel Talk was created in 2015, the Green Parrot Development lineup has grown significantly (along with our movie database). Everyone wants to talk about what they watch, so our team is dedicated to making movie-watching a collaborative experience.
